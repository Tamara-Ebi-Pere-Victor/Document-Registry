Document Registry

Welcome to the "Document Registry" DApp. This decentralized app runs on the Celo blockchain network and holds a registry of documents in a Solidity smart contract.
  • The registry keeps the hashes of the documents along with their publish date.
  • Contract owner can submit new documents to be stored on the blockchain.
  • Users can verify the existence of certain document in the registry.

Submit a Document

Contract owners can register (upload) new documents to the "Document Registry" smart contract on the Celo blockchain decentralized network.


Verify a Document

Blockchain users can verify documents by checking whether they exist in the "Document Registry" smart contract on the Celo blockchain decentralized network.
